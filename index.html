Get to know ME

(Caracas, 1989) My name is Jose Alberto, I'm a dedicated Full Stack Developer based in Barcelona, Spain. With a unique journey that blends a rich background in music, writing, and performance art with a newfound passion for the digital realm. After years of honing my skills as a musician, writer, and performer, I made the exciting decision to transition my artistic expertise into the world of technology.
My educational journey led me to dive into programming, where I discovered a profound love for front-end development and UX/UI design. My goal is to seamlessly merge my artistic sensibilities with cutting-edge technology, creating immersive and user-centric digital experiences.
Beyond the lines of code, I bring a creative perspective and an unwavering commitment to crafting visually stunning and intuitively functional interfaces. I am eager to contribute my diverse skill set to innovative projects and contribute my unique blend of artistic flair and technical expertise to the ever-evolving field of web development. Let's embark on this exciting journey together, merging the worlds of art and technology in captivating ways.
